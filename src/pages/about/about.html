About CBB

Ceria Bike Brunei (CBB)

The customer can rent the nearest available bicycle to them. Every bicycle will have GPS device installed so it can be tracked easily by its owner.

All bicycle will have the electrionic lock with it. The bicycle owner will send the bicycle to the nearest docking station and the customer can leave the bicycle to any docking station after they are done.
